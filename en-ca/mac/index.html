Cutling for Mac

Your clipboard, one hotkey away. Cutling lives in the menu bar and appears wherever your cursor is, so you can drop a saved snippet into any app without breaking your flow.

Download for Mac

Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple. Requires macOS 14 or later, Apple silicon or Intel.

Built for the desktop

Lives in the menu bar

Cutling sits quietly in your menu bar, out of the Dock and out of the way. Click the icon to see your saved cutlings and recent clipboard history.

Global hotkey

Press your shortcut from any app to open a floating picker right at your cursor. Rebind it to any key combination in settings.

Direct paste

Optionally let Cutling paste your pick straight into the app you were using. Off by default, and always your choice to enable.

Clipboard history

Everything you copy is captured automatically so nothing is lost. Save the ones worth keeping with a single click.

iCloud sync

Turn on iCloud to keep your saved cutlings in step across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Optional and private.

Private by design

No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your clipboard stays on your Mac, or in your own iCloud if you enable sync.
