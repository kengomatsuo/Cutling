Privacy Policy
Effective: February 22, 2026

Cutling does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Cutling is a keyboard extension that lets you save and quickly insert text snippets such as addresses, bank details, and other frequently used information. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Cutling collects no personal data whatsoever. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking, no advertising identifiers. The app does not contain any third-party analytics or tracking frameworks.

Your Snippets

All snippets you create are stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server. The developer has no ability to read, access, or retrieve your snippets.

Keyboard Extension

Cutling includes a custom keyboard extension that requires Full Access to read from your device's clipboard. This permission is used solely to let you paste and manage your saved snippets. The keyboard does not log, record, or transmit your keystrokes or clipboard contents to any server. All clipboard access occurs locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Cutling does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. No data is sent to external servers.

Children's Privacy

Cutling does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Because Cutling collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
