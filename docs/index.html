Privacy Policy
Effective: February 22, 2026

Cutling does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Overview

Cutling is a keyboard extension that lets you save and quickly insert text snippets such as addresses, bank details, and other frequently used information. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Cutling collects no personal data whatsoever. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage tracking, no advertising identifiers. The app does not contain any third-party analytics or tracking frameworks.

Your Snippets

All snippets you create are stored locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. Your snippet data is transferred directly between your devices through Apple's infrastructure. The developer has no ability to read, access, or retrieve your snippets.

Keyboard Extension

Cutling includes a custom keyboard extension that allows you to insert saved snippets into any app. The keyboard does not have Full Access enabled and does not access, log, or transmit anything you type. It only reads from the snippets you have saved within the Cutling app.

Third-Party Services

Cutling does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The only external service involved is Apple's iCloud, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Cutling does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Because Cutling collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
